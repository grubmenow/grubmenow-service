What is grubmenow?

Grubmenow is a place to order fresh, home cooked food. Read the consumer FAQs and How it Works for a quick overview.

Why should I sign up to be a chef?

Because you love to cook and ‘d not mind some fame and money :-)

What do I need to do after I sign up?

You list your menu a day in advance, get notified of orders, prepare the food, pack in containers we provide by the cut off time (7PM for dinner). The customer who placed order will pick up the food from your address between 6 and 9PM. See customer FAQ page and ‘How it Works’ for more information on the customer experience.

How do I list an item?

We have made it really simple for you. Just send a text/WhatsApp message to our customer service number 206 883 6876. Mention date and for each item you can serve on that date - item name, total quantity you can serve and price per item. We will take care of the rest. We recommend that you list your items a few days in advance so that more customers get a chance to order.

Is the food ready to eat?

Absolutely. If you wish, you can heat the food in microwave before you consume. Our food containers are microwave safe.

Why should I sign in to Facebook to place my order?

We require sign in so that we can process your order quickly. It’s also good to know that a real human is placing the order =) We provide your email ID and phone number to the chef so that they can contact you in case of any questions on the order.

Can I cancel my order?

Absolutely. You can cancel by 4PM on the date for which you ordered.

Will you deliver?

Sorry, at this time we do not provide delivery.

What if I am not happy with the quality of the food? Can I get a refund?

We are sorry that you are not happy. Contact us with your order details. We will do the best we can to resolve it for you, including providing a refund. We cannot refund for orders that you fail to pick up.

Where can I find food allergy information?

At this stage, we are unable to provide food allergy information. Our chefs prepare food in their kitchens where allergens might be present. If you have food allergies, please contact chef for ingredient information.

How do you check the quality of the food?

Food safety is very important to us. We take it very seriously. We take multiple steps to verify quality - we prescreen the cooking facility and food prepared by every chef before they get listed. We do random checks on the food as well as the cooking facility. We request every customer to rate chefs and provide feedback. We take every complaint seriously. When we are notified of any problems, we immediately take down the chef(s). See http://homefoodsafety.org/ for more information.
