About

I come from a lesser known Hadauti area of Rajasthan state in India. The food here is primarily Rajasthani with a touch of Gujrati and Madhya Pardesh taste. I specialize in North indian dishes with our staple diet being Chapati, Sabzi and Daal (Lentil). But I have picked up some South Indian dishes from friends, and I don't shy away from trying my own experiments. Some of them are instant hits, like the famous (among friends that is ;) ) Indian Pizza.

I love the fact that I can share my creations with more people via GrubMeNow. Looking forward to making new friends!
