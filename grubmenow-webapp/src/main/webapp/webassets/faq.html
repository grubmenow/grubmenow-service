Frequently Asked Questions

What is grubmenow?

Grubmenow is a place to order fresh, home cooked food. If you are looking to escape the mass produced food sold elsewhere, Grubmenow is where you should order your next lunch. Grubmenow ‘chefs’ prepare food at home. The food is made to order and is guaranteed to be fresh.

How does it work?

First you provide your delivery zip code. Then select if you want the food same day or next day. We will show you the items that match your search. We will also display the chef address information as well. Select one or more items listed by chef, enter your payment information and you are done. You will get a notification once the food is ready, you can pick it up in a 2 hour time window.

What locations do you currently serve?

We currently have chefs in Seattle downtown, Bellevue, Redmond and Kirkland zip codes. More locations will be added soon.

When can I order and when is the food ready?

Order by 5PM and pick up between 7 to 9PM.

Is the food ready to eat?

Absolutely. If you wish, you can heat the food in microwave before you consume. Our food containers are microwave safe.

Can I cancel my order?

Absolutely. You can cancel by 5PM on the date for which you ordered.

Will you deliver?

Sorry, at this time we do not provide delivery.

What if I am not happy with the quality of the food? Can I get a refund?

We are sorry that you are not happy. Contact us with your order details. We will do the best we can to resolve it for you, including providing a refund. We cannot refund for orders that you fail to pick up.

Where can I find food allergy information?

At this stage, we are unable to provide food allergy information. Our chefs prepare food in their kitchens where allergens might be present. If you have food allergies, please contact chef for ingredient information.

How do you check the quality of the food?

Food safety is very important to us. We take it very seriously. We take multiple steps to verify quality - we prescreen the cooking facility and food prepared by every chef before they get listed. We do random checks on the food as well as the cooking facility. We request every customer to rate chefs and provide feedback. We take every complaint seriously. When we are notified of any problems, we immediately take down the chef(s).
